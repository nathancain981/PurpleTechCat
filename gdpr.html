GDPR & Privacy

Any information you share is used only to respond to your enquiry or provide agreed support.

What I may collect?

  • Contact details you provide (such as name and email)
  • Details about your device or issue, when relevant to support

Your Data Rights

This page explains your rights. It tells you how your data is used and how you can ask for it.

What data we collect?

We only collect the information you give us. This is usually your name, email and message from the contact form.

Why we collect it?

We use your information so we can reply to you and help you. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with anyone unless the law says we must.

Your rights

You have rights over your data. These include:

  • Seeing the data we hold about you
  • Asking us to fix wrong information
  • Asking us to delete your data

Subject Access Request (SAR)

A SAR means you can ask for a copy of the personal data we hold about you. You can ask for this using the contact form.

Freedom of Information (FOI)

An FOI means you can ask for general information about how PurpleTechCat works. FOI is for information, not personal data.

We will reply to SAR and FOI requests within one month.

How to contact us?

If you want to make a SAR or FOI request, please use the contact form. We will explain things in a clear and friendly way.
