GDPR & Privacy

This page explains how your information is used.

What I may collect?

  • Your name and email (from the contact form)
  • Details about your device or issue, if needed for support

Why I collect your information?

I only use your information so I can reply to you and give the support you asked for.

I do not sell your data. I do not share it with anyone unless the law says I must.

Your Data Rights

You have rights over your personal data. These rights help you stay in control.

Your rights include

  • Seeing the data I hold about you
  • Asking me to fix wrong information
  • Asking me to delete your data
  • Asking how your data is used

Subject Access Request (SAR)

A SAR means you can ask for a copy of the personal data I hold about you.

You can make a SAR using the contact form. I will reply within one month.

Freedom of Information (FOI)

An FOI request lets you ask for general information about how PurpleTechCat works.

FOI is for information, not personal data.

How long I keep your data?

I only keep your information for as long as needed to provide support. After that, it is safely deleted.

How to contact me?

If you want to make a SAR or FOI request, please use the contact form. I will explain things in a clear and friendly way.
