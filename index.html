Welcome

PurpleTechCat is a free, home‑based IT support project for children, young people, adults and families in the L25 community.

What PurpleTechCat can do?

  • 🖥️ Hardware – fixing and setting up devices
  • 💿 Software – installs, updates, troubleshooting
  • 🌐 Networking – Wi‑Fi issues, slow internet, router setup
  • 🔒 Cybersecurity – passwords, scam awareness, staying safe online
  • 🎮 Gaming – setup, performance issues, safe online play
  • 📱 Devices – tablets, laptops, phones, smart home setup
  • ♿ Accessibility – making tech easier to use for all ages

What PurpleTechCat can’t do?

  • 🚫 No repairs that need specialist tools (e.g. soldering, screen replacements)
  • 🚫 No electrical work beyond normal device setup
  • 🚫 No work that requires business insurance or professional certification
  • 🚫 No handling of sensitive personal data (bank details, medical info, etc.)
  • 🚫 No emergency support or safeguarding investigations
  • 🚫 No paid work – PurpleTechCat is a free community project
  • 🚫 No services that put health, safety or privacy at risk
