Frequently Asked Questions

PurpleTechCat – FAQ

What is PurpleTechCat?

PurpleTechCat is my personal project where I offer free, friendly IT help for the L25 community. I explain things in simple language and help make technology less stressful.

What can you help with?

I can help with slow devices, Wi‑Fi issues, email problems, passwords, new device setup, online safety, error messages, file organisation, gaming issues, and general tech worries.

Do you support gaming?

Yes — I can help with console setup, connection issues, parental controls, online safety, and basic troubleshooting for PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo and PC gaming.

Do you support primary school devices?

Yes — I can help with school laptops, tablets, logins, homework platforms, safe browsing, and making sure children can use their devices confidently and safely.

Do you help with insured devices?

Yes — I can check what’s wrong, help you gather proof for a claim, explain the issue in simple words, and help set up a replacement device. I don’t provide insurance myself.

What can’t you do?

I don’t repair broken screens or hardware, replace parts, visit homes, contact insurance companies, remove parental controls, or work on business servers.

Is your help really free?

Yes — PurpleTechCat is a free community service. I enjoy helping people feel more confident with technology.

When will you reply?

I reply during my support hours: Tue–Thu 6pm–10pm, Sat–Sun 7am–10am. If you message outside these times, you’ll see “Offline – I will reply during my support hours.”

How do you book support?

Use the booking button on the Home Page to choose a time that works for you. All support is online.

Is my information safe?

Yes — I only ask for the details I need to help you. I don’t store or share your information.
