Frequently Asked Questions

PurpleTechCat – FAQ

What is PurpleTechCat?

PurpleTechCat is my personal project where I offer free, friendly IT help for the L25 community.

What can you help with?

I can help with slow devices, Wi‑Fi issues, email problems, passwords, new device setup, online safety, and more.

Do you support gaming?

Yes — consoles, PC gaming, parental controls, and connection issues.

Do you support primary school devices?

Yes — laptops, tablets, logins, homework platforms, and safe browsing.

Do you help with insured devices?

Yes — I help diagnose issues and prepare information for claims.

What can’t you do?

I don’t repair hardware, visit homes, or work on business servers.

Is your help really free?

Yes — PurpleTechCat is a free community service.

When will you reply?

I reply during my support hours: Tue–Wed 6pm–10pm, Thu 8pm–10pm, Sat–Sun 7am–10am.

How do you book support?

Use the booking button above to choose a time.

Is my information safe?

Yes — I only ask for the details needed to help you.
